Our Identity

With a proud legacy spanning generations, Kantipur Open Rover Ranger Crew stands as one of Nepal's oldest continuously active Rover Scout Crews. Guided by the Scout Promise and Law, we strive to develop responsible leaders through adventure, service, and lifelong learning. Through volunteerism, environmental stewardship, disaster response, youth development, and community initiatives, we remain committed to creating a lasting positive impact on Nepalese society while contributing to the vision of building a better world.

Our Mission

To develop self-reliant, responsible citizens by providing dynamic, value-based programs centered around outdoor survival, leadership roles, and impactful global citizenship.

Our Vision

To craft an elite brotherhood and sisterhood of scouts who continually push structural boundaries, seek new heights of adventure, and leave the world better than they found it.
